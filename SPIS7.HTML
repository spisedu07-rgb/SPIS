About Us

Welcome to Sardar Patel International School, where we are committed to academic excellence and holistic development. Our school nurtures students to excel in academics, sports, and extracurricular activities while building strong values.

Academics

  • Primary School: Grades 1 - 5, focusing on creativity and basics
  • Middle School: Grades 6 - 8, fostering curiosity and teamwork
  • High School: Grades 9 - 12, preparing for college and careers
